Enies Lobby Arc

The Enies Lobby Arc is the sixteenth story arc of the manga and anime One Piece, and the third story arc of the Water 7 Saga. Enies Lobby was a government-controlled stronghold in Paradise. The Straw Hats, Sogeking, the Franky Family, and the shipwrights of the Galley-La Company arrive at Enies Lobby aboard the Rocketman to rescue Nico Robin and Franky. Standing in their way, however, are the forces of the World Government, including its deadly assassination unit, CP9. A massive battle ensues between the two sides when Luffy declares war against the World Government.

Arabasta Arc

The Arabasta Arc is the eleventh story arc of the manga and anime One Piece, and both the fifth and last story arc of the Arabasta Saga. The Arabasta Kingdom is a desert kingdom in Paradise. The Straw Hats finally reach the desert kingdom and begin their race across the land to reach Alubarna before a massive war can break out between the kingdom's royal guards and the rebels. However, the true perpetrator of the war, Mr. 0 (Crocodile), and his remaining Baroque Works officer agents stand in the way of the Straw Hats reaching their goal.

Impel Down Arc

The Impel Down Arc is the twenty-first story arc of the manga and anime One Piece, and the third story arc of the Summit War Saga. Impel Down, also known as the Underwater Prison and the Great Prison, is a government-controlled stronghold in Paradise together with Marine Headquarters and Enies Lobby. Luffy puts finding his crew on hold to rescue his brother Ace from the underwater prison. With Boa Hancock's help, he manages to infiltrate the facility. However, fulfilling his objective and escaping the hellish prison might be easier said than done.
